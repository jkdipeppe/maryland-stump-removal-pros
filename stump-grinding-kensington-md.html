Kensington Stump Grinding Questions

How much does stump grinding cost in Kensington, MD?

Stump grinding cost in Kensington depends on the stump's diameter, the wood's hardness, how many stumps you have, and accessibility. Most single-stump jobs are very affordable and multiple stumps cost less per stump. Call or text 301-355-3769 with a photo for a free Kensington quote.

Do you offer stump removal in Kensington?

Yes. We provide both stump grinding and full stump removal throughout Kensington and the rest of Montgomery County. Grinding is faster and more affordable; full removal extracts the entire stump and root ball when you're building or replanting in the same spot.

How soon can you grind a stump in Kensington?

We schedule Kensington jobs quickly — often within a few days, and faster for emergencies. Text us a photo of your stump and we'll get you a price and a time.

How deep do you grind a stump?

We typically grind stumps to 8–12 inches below the surrounding grade — deep enough to plant grass, lay sod, or install new landscaping over the area. If you're planting a new tree or shrub in the same location, let us know and we can grind deeper or recommend full stump removal instead.

Can I plant grass after stump grinding?

Yes. Once the stump is ground below grade we can backfill the hole with the wood grindings or with clean topsoil so the area is level and ready to seed. For the best lawn results we recommend backfilling with topsoil before seeding, since fresh wood chips can temporarily tie up nitrogen in the soil.

What happens to the wood chips and debris?

That's your choice. We can spread the grindings as natural mulch around your beds and tree rings, or we can haul everything away and backfill with topsoil for a clean, level finish. Either way we always leave the work area cleaner than we found it.
