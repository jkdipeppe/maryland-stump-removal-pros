Common Questions

Stump Grinding FAQ

How much does stump grinding cost in Maryland?

Cost depends on the stump's diameter, the wood's hardness, how many stumps there are, and how accessible they are. Most single-stump residential jobs are very affordable, and multiple stumps cost less per stump. We give free, upfront quotes — just call or text 301-355-3769 with a photo.

What's the difference between stump grinding and stump removal?

For most people they mean the same thing. Stump grinding chips the stump down to 8–12 inches below ground and leaves the deeper roots to decompose — it's fast, affordable, and the right choice for almost every yard. If you want all the wood chips cleared out so you can backfill with topsoil, sod, or a new tree, we add our cleanup & haul away service.

Can I plant grass after the stump is ground down?

Yes. After grinding we can backfill with the grindings or haul them away and add topsoil so you can seed grass or lay sod right away. Planting a new tree in the exact spot? Our cleanup & haul away service clears the chips so you've got clean soil to plant into.
