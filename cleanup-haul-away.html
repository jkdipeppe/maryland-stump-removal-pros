Cleanup & Haul Away

Stump Grinding Cleanup & Haul Away in Montgomery County, MD

Stump grinding leaves behind a mound of wood shavings and mulch. With our cleanup & haul away service, we take all of it away and leave you a clean, open hole — ready for you to fill with whatever you'd like, whether that's topsoil and sod, fresh dirt, or a brand-new tree.

What's included

  • Complete removal of all wood shavings and grindings
  • Mulch and debris hauled off your property
  • A clean, open hole ready for backfill
  • Optional topsoil backfill so you can seed or sod right away
  • A tidy, swept work area when we leave

Why haul the shavings away?

By default, stump grinding leaves the chips in place as mulch — which is fine if you just want the stump gone. But fresh wood chips aren't great soil to plant into: they temporarily tie up nitrogen and settle over time. Hauling them away gives you a clean cavity to fill with exactly what you want, so your new grass, plant, or tree gets the best possible start.

Fill it with whatever you like

Once we've cleared the grindings, the spot is yours. Drop in clean topsoil and lay sod for an instant lawn, seed it with grass, plant a new tree or shrub, or just level it with dirt. We can backfill with topsoil for you on request, or leave it open for your landscaper.

Pairs with grinding & root removal

Cleanup & haul away is the perfect add-on to stump grinding, and it pairs naturally with root removal when you want the whole area cleared and restored. Just let us know when you book and we'll quote it all together.
