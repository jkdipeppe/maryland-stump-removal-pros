Root Removal

Root Removal in Montgomery County, MD

Surface and shallow roots can buckle driveways, crack walkways and patios, threaten foundations, and create tripping hazards across your lawn. Our root removal service targets those problem roots directly — clearing the trouble while protecting the trees and landscaping you want to keep.

Signs you need root removal

  • Cracked or lifted driveway, sidewalk, or patio
  • Exposed roots making mowing or walking hazardous
  • Roots creeping toward your foundation or utility lines
  • Bumpy, uneven lawn from shallow root systems

How we remove roots

We trace the problem roots and carefully cut and remove the offending sections, working to preserve the health of surrounding trees and plants wherever possible. Where roots tie back to a stump, root removal pairs naturally with stump grinding for a complete fix.

Protecting your property

Before any work we recommend calling 811 (Miss Utility) to mark underground lines. We work carefully around hardscaping, irrigation, and healthy trees so the cure isn't worse than the problem.

Pairs well with

Root removal is often done alongside stump grinding or full stump removal. If you've got both a stump and problem roots, we'll handle them together and quote it as one job.
