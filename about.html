About Us

Your Local Montgomery County Stump Specialists

Maryland Stump Removal Pros is a locally operated stump grinding and removal company serving homeowners and businesses throughout Montgomery County, Maryland. We're not a giant tree-service outfit that treats stumps as an afterthought — stumps are what we do, and we do them right.

When a tree comes down, you're often left with a stubborn stump that's a tripping hazard, an eyesore, and a magnet for pests and regrowth. We show up with the right equipment, grind or remove it cleanly, and leave your yard ready to use again. No upsells, no runaround — just a fair price and honest work.

Why homeowners choose us

  • We're local. Based right here in Montgomery County, so we know the area and we show up.
  • Fully insured. Liability coverage on every job protects you and your property.
  • Fair, upfront pricing. Your quote is your price — no surprises.
  • We clean up. Grindings spread as mulch or hauled away, and the site left tidy.
  • Easy to reach. Call or text a photo and get a fast, free quote.

What we handle

From a single backyard stump to dozens across a commercial property or HOA, we take on jobs of every size. Our services include stump grinding and removal, root removal, and cleanup & haul away, plus topsoil backfill and emergency storm-damage response.

Where we work

We serve all of Montgomery County, including Bethesda, Rockville, Potomac, Gaithersburg, Germantown, Silver Spring, and many more. See our full service areas.
