Stump Removal

Stump Removal in Montgomery County, MD

Sometimes grinding isn't enough — you need the entire stump and root system gone. Our stump removal service fully extracts the stump and root ball from the ground, leaving a clean site ready for construction, a patio, a new tree, or a complete landscape redo.

When to choose full removal

  • You're building, paving, or pouring concrete over the spot
  • You want to plant a new tree in the exact same location
  • You want every trace of the old tree gone, roots and all
  • The roots are interfering with utilities or hardscaping

How removal works

Unlike grinding, removal extracts the stump and major root ball from the soil. This leaves a larger hole, which we backfill with soil so the area is level and usable. Removal takes more time and effort than grinding, so it costs more — but it's the most thorough option when complete elimination matters.

Removal vs. grinding

For most homeowners who simply want the stump gone and the space back, stump grinding is faster and more affordable. Choose full removal when the roots themselves are the problem or you're reusing the exact spot. We're happy to walk you through which makes sense for your project.

Cleanup & restoration

We haul away the extracted stump and debris and backfill the hole so you're left with a clean, level area — not a crater. Need the surrounding roots dealt with too? See our root removal service.
